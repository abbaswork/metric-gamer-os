# Metric: Car Roster
**Genre:** Racing
**Type:** Quantitative
**Direction:** Delight — 5 = largest roster, 1 = smallest roster

## Why This Matters

Car count is the first number players cite when comparing racing games. It's used as a quick signal for content value and longevity — a bigger roster means more to discover, more to unlock, and more reason to return. Worth noting: community research from a NeoGAF open letter to racing game producers found that players actually ranked track variety above car lists when asked what matters most. Car roster still belongs in the set, but it likely lands below track variety in terms of how strongly it drives player satisfaction.

## Sources

- **NeoGAF: "An open letter to racing games producers"** — track variety ranked above car lists by fans; car count still matters but track variety is the stronger signal (live source, confirmed via web search)
- **r/patientgamers** and **r/racing** (training data) — car count is a standard comparison point across recommendation threads
- **r/granturismo** and **r/forza** (training data) — roster size is a regular talking point in comparisons between the two franchises
- **Steam user reviews** (training data) — "only X cars" is a shorthand criticism for thin rosters that appears across multiple titles

**Threshold anchors (approximate, based on launch rosters):**
- GT7: ~420 cars
- Forza Horizon 5: ~500 cars
- Need for Speed Heat: ~127 cars
- Grid Legends: ~130 cars
- Dirt Rally 2.0: ~50 cars
- Assetto Corsa Competizione: ~30 cars (GT3/GT4 class-specific, not a general roster)

## Rubric

| Score | Threshold | What This Means for You |
|---|---|---|
| 5 | 200+ cars | If you're into a specific niche like JDM cars, you'll find plenty of options. You won't feel like you're cycling through the same handful. |
| 4 | 100–199 cars | You'll spend a decent amount of time before you feel like you've seen everything. It may not be the biggest roster, but it doesn't feel thin. |
| 3 | 50–99 cars | Variety across classes works for a while. Stick to one category and the options run out faster than expected. |
| 2 | 20–49 cars | The ceiling on variety comes up fast. Longer sessions keep putting the same cars in front of you. |
| 1 | Under 20 cars | The car selection becomes a frustration early. There isn't much to discover after the first few hours. |
