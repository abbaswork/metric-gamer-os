| 4 | Campaign Structure | Qualitative | Delight |
| 5 | Enemy Variety | Qualitative | Delight |

## Sub-genre Swap Table

| Sub-genre | Swap Out | Swap In |
|---|---|---|
| Multiplayer | Level Design, Campaign Structure, Enemy Variety | Map Design, Netcode / Server Quality, Progression System |
| Free | Enemy Variety | Monetisation Model |

When the context is multiplayer, the three SP-specific metrics become irrelevant — there is no campaign, no enemy AI to evaluate, and encounter spaces are judged by different criteria. Replace all three with Map Design, Netcode / Server Quality, and Progression System.

When the game is free-to-play (SP context), Enemy Variety is replaced by Monetisation Model. Free SP shooters are uncommon, but when they exist the business model is a primary part of the player experience. For free multiplayer shooters, the Progression System metric already covers monetisation concerns — no additional swap is needed.
