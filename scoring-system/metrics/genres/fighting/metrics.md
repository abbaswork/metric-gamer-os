# Genre: Fighting
## Default Metrics (Single Player)

| # | Metric | Type | Direction |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Roster Size | Quantitative | Delight |
| 2 | Character Identity | Qualitative | Delight |
| 3 | Mechanical Depth | Qualitative | Delight |
| 4 | Mode Variety | Qualitative | Delight |
| 5 | AI Quality | Qualitative | Complaint |

## Sub-genre Swap Table

| Sub-genre | Swap Out | Swap In |
|---|---|---|
| Multiplayer / Competitive | Mode Variety, AI Quality | Netcode Quality, Competitive Balance |
| Free | AI Quality | Monetisation Model |

When the context is multiplayer or competitive play, Mode Variety and AI Quality become irrelevant — you are fighting human opponents and single player content depth is not being evaluated. Replace both with Netcode Quality and Competitive Balance.

When the game is free-to-play, AI Quality is replaced by Monetisation Model. Free fighting games are typically live-service and online-focused — how the game monetises its roster and content is a core part of the player experience.
