5 = The world is large, varied, and worth exploring for its own sake, with shortcuts, hidden routes, and distinct environments that give you reasons to go off-route, minimal invisible walls.
4 = The world is large with strong variety, but free exploration is restricted in places, or a portion of the world exists mainly as scenery.
3 = The tracks or world are a reasonable size, but the environments repeat enough that things start to blur together and there isn't much to find off the established path.
2 = Track count is thin or the open world isn't really open, with large sections closed off and environments that look the same.
1 = There are only a handful of generic, interchangeable tracks, or a world that exists purely as a backdrop with no reason to go off the planned route.
