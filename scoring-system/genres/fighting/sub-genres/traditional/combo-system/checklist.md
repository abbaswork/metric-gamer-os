# Checklist: Combo System
**Genre:** Fighting
**Sub-genre:** Traditional

---

5 = You can land combos and deal real damage from the start, putting time into harder sequences pays off without needing to be perfect under match pressure, there's enough space between simple and advanced to develop your own style
4 = Clear ways in with real improvement paths, some characters have more to explore than others, the difference between simple and advanced is noticeable without being discouraging
3 = Most characters have one main combo with small variations, it works without giving you much reason to go deeper than the basics
2 = Either landing combos in a real match is too demanding for most players to convert reliably, or the system is so simple there's one sequence and nothing to explore
1 = The combo system doesn't reward learning, either too hard to land anything in a match or too simple for anything to develop
