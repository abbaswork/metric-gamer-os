# Checklist: Competitive Balance
**Genre:** Fighting
**Sub-genre:** Traditional

---

5 = Your main is a personal choice, off-meta picks are a real option at higher ranks, the tier list exists but the gaps aren't wide enough to punish character loyalty
4 = A few clear outliers at the top but off-meta picks still show up in competitive results, the meta shapes character select at the highest level without defining the experience below it
3 = Clear tier clustering that you start to feel at higher ranks, playing off-meta is possible but carries a cost you'll notice
2 = A narrow dominant tier, character variety collapses in ranked play, patches have been slow or made things worse
1 = The live meta is broken, one character or strategy wins at rates that remove any real competition across the board
