# Checklist: Presentation
**Genre:** Fighting
**Sub-genre:** Traditional

---

5 = The music gives the game a distinct atmosphere, your character has a personality you enjoy engaging with, the cosmetic system gives you a reason to invest in your main aside from winning
4 = Clear visual identity with strong highlights, effects are generally readable, sound design is solid with a few standout moments
3 = The game looks fine without looking distinct, characters are identifiable, sound design does its job without pulling you in
2 = Visual clutter makes the screen hard to read at key moments, sound design is weak or generic, hits don't confirm with confidence
1 = The presentation gets in the way of playing, the screen is regularly hard to parse, audio feedback is absent or misleading
