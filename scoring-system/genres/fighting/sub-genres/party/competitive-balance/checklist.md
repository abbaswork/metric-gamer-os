# Checklist: Competitive Balance
**Genre:** Fighting
**Sub-genre:** Party Fighter

---

5 = No one starts with a design advantage, base mechanics are the same for every player, and outcomes are determined by play and physics not by what anyone picked
4 = Effectively equal, minor differences exist but the physics variability is enough that they don't produce consistent advantages across sessions
3 = Small differences that experienced players can find, but the chaos covers for most of it and most sessions don't surface them
2 = Differences are noticeable enough that experienced players exploit them consistently, the physics chaos isn't enough to compensate
1 = The design advantages are such that what or who you pick matters more than how you actually play, and the chaos doesn't level the playing field
