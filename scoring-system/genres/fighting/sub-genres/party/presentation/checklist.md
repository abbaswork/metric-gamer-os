# Checklist: Presentation
**Genre:** Fighting
**Sub-genre:** Party Fighter

---

5 = You can tell what kind of game it is from across the room, you can track yourself through a four-player pile, and the people watching without a controller are entertained
4 = The premise is clear and characters are mostly trackable, though dense moments occasionally make the action harder to read and spectators can follow without always being drawn in
3 = The presentation works at lower player counts but full four-player chaos starts to strain the visual clarity, characters are distinct on their own but harder to follow in the pile
2 = You regularly lose yourself in a four-player pile and the visual style doesn't clearly communicate what kind of game this is
1 = You can't track what's happening in chaos, the visual style communicates nothing about the premise, and there's nothing to hold the attention of people who aren't playing
