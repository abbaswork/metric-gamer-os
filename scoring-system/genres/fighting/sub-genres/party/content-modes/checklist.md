# Checklist: Content & Modes
**Genre:** Fighting
**Sub-genre:** Party Fighter

---

5 = Enough map and mode variety that groups are still finding different situations across multiple sessions, the maps change how play unfolds not just how it looks
4 = Strong variety that holds across a few sessions, some content goes unexplored but it's there when the group wants something different
3 = Enough variety for a single session but repeat sessions start to feel familiar, maps and modes don't create enough different situations to stay fresh
2 = The game's content is mostly exhausted in a single session, maps look different without creating different play situations
1 = One or two maps with a single mode, but there's nothing to come back to after the first session
