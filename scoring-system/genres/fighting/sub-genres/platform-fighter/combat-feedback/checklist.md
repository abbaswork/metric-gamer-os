# Checklist: Combat Feedback
**Genre:** Fighting
**Sub-genre:** Platform Fighter

---

5 = Hits feel physical and distinct, kill moves feel categorically different from regular hits, you know a stock is gone before the animation confirms it, movement is consistent enough to plan off-stage, and knockback reads at the moment of contact
4 = The aerial system mostly communicates clearly, kill confirmation is present but occasionally less distinct than it should be, knockback is readable in most situations with some exceptions, and inputs register reliably
3 = The mechanics work but don't fully communicate, hits confirm without the weight that tells you what they did, movement feels functional rather than good, and you're finding out what happened from the animation more than from how it felt
2 = Off-stage play feels like guesswork, kills feel like the game's decision rather than yours, knockback is unreliable enough that you can't predict outcomes, and inputs occasionally don't register in aerial sequences
1 = The aerial system fights back before any opponent does, inputs drop, movement is unpredictable, kill confirmation is absent, and hits don't distinguish from whiffs
