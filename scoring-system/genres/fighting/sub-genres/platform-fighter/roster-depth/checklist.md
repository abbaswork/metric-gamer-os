# Checklist: Roster Depth
**Genre:** Fighting
**Sub-genre:** Platform Fighter

---

5 = Picking a main is a real identity decision, the cast spans enough archetypes to produce genuinely different experiences, off-meta picks are competitive choices
4 = Strong variety across archetypes, most players find someone to genuinely invest in, a few characters feel redundant but the cast has personality
3 = The roster covers expected archetypes without distinction, picking a character feels practical rather than expressive, a few weapon types or approaches dominate in practice
2 = Characters share kits closely enough that playing different ones doesn't produce meaningfully different experiences, viability clusters around a few picks, the roster isn't a reason to stay
1 = Characters are barely distinguishable or so few that player identity can't form around them, the cast is the game's most obvious weakness from the moment you open character select
