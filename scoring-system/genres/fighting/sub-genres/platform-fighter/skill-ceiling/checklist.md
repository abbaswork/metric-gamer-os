# Checklist: Skill Ceiling
**Genre:** Fighting
**Sub-genre:** Platform Fighter

---

5 = The ceiling is genuinely high, aerial reads and off-stage play take a long time to master, entry friction is low enough that new players are landing hits within the first match rather than dying to the physics
4 = The mastery ceiling is real and rewards long-term investment, new players take a little longer to find their footing but get there quickly with guidance
3 = The ceiling exists but the game doesn't surface it well, casual players can plateau without realising there's more to learn, entry friction is high
2 = Either the skill ceiling runs out quickly or getting started is too punishing, the game doesn't deliver on both ends
1 = Shallow mastery and a hostile entry experience, the physics are the obstacle, clearing them doesn't reveal much depth on the other side
