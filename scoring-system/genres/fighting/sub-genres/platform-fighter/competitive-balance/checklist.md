# Checklist: Competitive Balance
**Genre:** Fighting
**Sub-genre:** Platform Fighter

---

5 = The tier list exists but the gap between tiers is narrow enough that who you pick is a style choice rather than a necessity, and off-stage is two-sided with recoveries that have real options and edgeguards that have counterplay
4 = A clear top tier exists but it isn't mandatory, you can win with off-meta picks if your play is there, and off-stage is mostly two-sided with a couple of options that are notably stronger but not dominant
3 = Tier clustering is real and you'll feel the cost of going off-meta at higher ranks, off-stage has dominant routes that become recognisable patterns, though counterplay still exists
2 = The viable pool is narrow enough that competitive play converges on the same picks, and off-stage increasingly has dominant options where the defending side has limited answers
1 = One character cluster or toolkit is dominant enough to remove meaningful choice, off-stage has one-sided solutions with no viable counterplay, and the imbalance is known and unfixed
