# Checklist: Stage Design
**Genre:** Fighting
**Sub-genre:** Platform Fighter

---

5 = Stage layouts vary enough that matchup dynamics shift noticeably by stage, the competitive pool is broad enough that stage selection is a real strategic decision, hazard stages feel playful rather than arbitrary in casual play
4 = Strong stage variety with a solid competitive pool, a few stages are clearly weaker but the selection is healthy, hazard stages work in casual contexts
3 = Platform layouts repeat enough that most stages feel like minor variants, the competitive pool narrows quickly, hazard stages are either always on or always off by convention
2 = Few viable stages with minimal layout variety, the competitive pool collapses to one or two stages, most hazard stages feel unfair rather than fun
1 = Stages are effectively identical or so unfair that play collapses to a single arena, stage select is cosmetic
