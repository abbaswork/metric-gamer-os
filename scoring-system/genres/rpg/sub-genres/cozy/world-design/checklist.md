# Checklist: World Design
**Genre:** RPG
**Sub-genre:** Cozy

---

5 = A distinctive art style and soundtrack work together to create a world that's genuinely inviting to explore, with multiple areas worth returning to for their own sake.
4 = Strong art and sound design with good variety across areas, though one zone clearly got less attention than the rest.
3 = The art style is pleasant but not distinctive, with music that blends into the background rather than adding to the atmosphere.
2 = Inconsistent art or a repetitive soundtrack that fades to background noise, with a map that offers little new to look at after the first few hours.
1 = A flat or mismatched visual style and a bare soundtrack leave a world with nothing worth exploring beyond where a quest marker tells you to go.
