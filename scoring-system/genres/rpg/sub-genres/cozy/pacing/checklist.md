# Checklist: Pacing
**Genre:** RPG
**Sub-genre:** Cozy

---

5 = The game respects your time with flexible session structure and smooth progression that never forces grinding or urgency.
4 = A stamina or day-length system exists but is generous enough that it rarely cuts a session short before you've accomplished what you set out to do.
3 = Stamina and day-length limits are noticeable enough that you'll start timing your tasks to the clock, though the rhythm is manageable once you understand it.
2 = Energy caps and short day lengths regularly cut sessions short mid-task, and at least one progression stage grinds to a halt before things move again.
1 = Tight stamina caps and rigid day timers cut sessions short so gameplay feels like a chore.
