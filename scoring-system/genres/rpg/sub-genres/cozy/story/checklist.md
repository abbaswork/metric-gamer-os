# Checklist: Story
**Genre:** RPG
**Sub-genre:** Cozy

---

5 = A strong cast of distinct, well-written characters each has a clear arc that develops over time, with dialogue that feels personal, memorable, and genuinely human.
4 = Most characters have real arcs and memorable dialogue, though a few feel underdeveloped.
3 = Characters have backstories and the story has direction, but the writing rarely feels distinctive or surprising.
2 = Most characters are defined by a single trait, with a story too thin to give your progress any emotional weight.
1 = Characters are interchangeable with no real backstory, and nothing in the writing gives you a reason to care.
