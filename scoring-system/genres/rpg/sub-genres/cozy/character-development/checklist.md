# Checklist: Character Development
**Genre:** RPG
**Sub-genre:** Cozy

---

5 = A varied cast of memorable characters offers meaningful relationships that unlock new dialogue, events, and story content over time.
4 = Most characters have distinct personalities with real relationship progression, though a few feel more like background filler than people worth investing in.
3 = Characters are distinguishable and relationships are tracked, but deepening them rarely unlocks anything beyond a new line of dialogue.
2 = Most NPCs feel interchangeable after a few conversations, with relationship meters that go up without anything meaningful changing behind them.
1 = NPCs feel interchangeable, with little meaningful interaction or relationship progression.
