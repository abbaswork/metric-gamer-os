# Checklist: Gameplay
**Genre:** RPG
**Sub-genre:** Cozy

---

5 = Every activity feeds into multiple systems at once, with new unlocks and recipes arriving often enough that there's always something to look forward to.
4 = A varied loop with a steady unlock pace, though one activity type lags behind the others in terms of meaningful payoff.
3 = The loop functions but rewards arrive on a predictable, flat schedule, with stretches of play that feel more like maintenance than progress.
2 = Several sessions pass with no new unlock or system to explore, leaving you repeating the same handful of actions with nothing new in sight.
1 = A bare loop of one or two repeated actions with no escalation, unlocks, or goal-driven thread to pull you forward.
