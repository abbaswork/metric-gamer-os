# Checklist: Customisation
**Genre:** RPG
**Sub-genre:** Cozy

---

5 = Deep decorating and building systems with enough structural freedom and item variety that no two players' spaces look alike.
4 = Strong furnishing freedom and a solid item catalogue, though one structural layer like exterior layout or floor plans remains locked or limited.
3 = Decoration is possible with some placement freedom, but a modest item catalogue and limited structural options mean spaces rarely look dramatically different.
2 = Customisation is mostly grid-locked placement from a small item set, with little room to build anything that looks uniquely yours.
1 = There's no meaningful decorating system to speak of, leaving your space looking the same as everyone else's.
