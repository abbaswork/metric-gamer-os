# Metric: Content & Game Modes
**Genre:** Sports
**Sub-genre:** Football
**Type:** Qualitative (community consensus)
**Direction:** Delight — 5 = multiple distinct, well-supported modes that each justify the purchase on their own, 1 = a single mode with no depth or alternative ways to play

## Why This Matters

research.md's Trends data shows players searching for specific modes by name, not just the base game — "ea fc 26 world cup mode" and "ea fc 26 career mode" both register real, distinct search activity, and "world cup video games" spikes +700% on top of the +450% rise in "fifa video games" generally. That's a different shape of demand than just genre interest: players are choosing a football game based on which specific mode it offers, and a tie-in mode (like EA Sports FC 26's World Cup Mode) can drive a purchase on its own merits independent of the base game's on-pitch feel.

This metric exists because the genre has converged on offering several structurally different ways to play within one purchase — Career Mode (single-player narrative progression), Ultimate Team (collection/squad-building meta-game), Pro Clubs (cooperative online), and now licensed tournament tie-ins — and a game's mode roster is frequently the deciding factor between two otherwise similar releases.

## Rubric

| Score | Description | What This Means for You |
|---|---|---|
| 5 | Multiple structurally different modes (career/management, collection/squad-building, cooperative or online), each with enough depth and support to justify playing on its own. Tie-in or seasonal modes (tournament events) add real, distinct content rather than a coat of paint. | Whatever way you like to play football games, there's a fully-built mode here for it — not just a menu option. |
| 4 | Several distinct modes exist and most are well-supported, but one is clearly thinner than the others or feels like an afterthought. | Most of what you want is here. One mode feels bolted on. |
| 3 | A couple of modes exist, but only one has real depth — the rest are shallow variations or reskins of the same core loop. | You'll mostly play one mode. The others are there to pad the box, not to be played seriously. |
| 2 | Effectively one playable mode with minor cosmetic variations presented as separate options. | It's one game wearing a few different menu labels. |
| 1 | A single mode with no meaningful structure or alternative way to engage with the game. | What you see in the first hour is the whole game. |
