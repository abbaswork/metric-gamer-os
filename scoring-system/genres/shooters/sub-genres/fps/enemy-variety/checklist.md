# Checklist: Enemy Variety
**Genre:** Shooters
**Sub-genre:** FPS

---

5 = Each enemy type demands a different approach, making weapon choice, positioning, and timing a constant tactical decision throughout.
4 = Strong variety with meaningful behavioral differences across most types, though a few are similar enough that they rarely demand a real change in approach.
3 = Enemy types vary in health and damage output, but you'll carry the same approach through the whole game.
2 = Most enemies behave the same way despite looking different, and the game stops introducing new types well before the halfway point.
1 = One enemy type dressed up in different skins, the game never asks you to change how you play.
