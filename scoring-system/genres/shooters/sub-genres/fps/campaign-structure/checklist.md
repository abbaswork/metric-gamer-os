# Checklist: Campaign Structure
**Genre:** Shooters
**Sub-genre:** FPS

---

5 = Every mission feels fresh, introducing new ideas and memorable moments without falling into repetitive filler.
4 = Well-paced with genuine variety, though a few sections lean too hard on one environment or encounter type.
3 = Missions change environments and objectives, but the underlying encounter structure stays the same throughout.
2 = The campaign leans heavily on one environment type or encounter pattern, stopping introducing new ideas well before the halfway point.
1 = A corridor with enemies, no structural variation, no moment distinct from another.
