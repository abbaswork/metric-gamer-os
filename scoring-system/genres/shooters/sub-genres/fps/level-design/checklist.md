# Checklist: Level Design
**Genre:** Shooters
**Sub-genre:** FPS

---

5 = Every combat arena feels distinct, with varied layouts and meaningful positioning that keep firefights engaging.
4 = Most encounter spaces give you real room to work, though a few sections lean too heavily on linear corridors.
3 = The levels are functional but generic, with cover that rarely creates positioning decisions worth making.
2 = Corridors and narrow rooms dominate, leaving firefights feeling the same no matter where you are in the campaign.
1 = Every room plays identically, with no positional variety and nothing in the level design worth engaging with.
