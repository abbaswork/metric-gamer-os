## Campaign 1 — Site Foundation
*Goal 2 | 4 weeks | Start: June 14 | Go-live: July 12, 2026 | Submission deadline: July 10*

### Overview
The game page structure is the single biggest blocker on Goals 2 and 3. Ranked list pages pull duplicate content from game pages, causing game pages to be de-indexed. The heading structure is inaccessible. EEAT enforcement rules — alt tags, automated FAQ, heading templates — do not exist. The rubric methodology is invisible across game pages, ranked lists, and the About page. No new content publishing begins until these structural issues are resolved. This is the highest-priority campaign in month 1.

AdSense strategy is included as a placeholder section below. Tasks will be defined by the SEO specialist following the meeting week of June 16 and confirmed by June 21.

### Strategy
Dev owns the structural fixes in strict priority order: duplicate content first (actively causing de-indexing), then heading structure and EEAT enforcement, then rubric visibility. Content provides the copy layers Dev needs to implement. The SEO specialist fills in the AdSense section after her June 16 meeting — point values for those tasks are assigned and published by June 21.

No new game pages are published until tasks 1.1–1.7 are complete and verified on the live site.

Content work begins once the site structure from Campaign 1 is in progress. Campaign 3 covers EEAT rewrites of existing pages, meta title and description fixes, affiliate account setup, and keyword research for the two pillar verticals. The AdSense specialist's confirmed action list from Campaign 1 feeds directly into this campaign's content tasks. EEAT rewrites come before new content — fixing existing pages extracts more value than publishing new ones and removes the drag from pages suppressing the domain. Affiliate accounts are set up at the start of the campaign so every new piece published carries links from day one. Keyword research and rubric completion for both pillar verticals happen in parallel with rewrites so that by go-live, cluster content work in month 2 can begin immediately.
